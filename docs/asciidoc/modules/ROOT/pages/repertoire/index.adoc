Active study of works in the performing repertoire.

== Approach

Repertoire study focuses on:

* **Form** — Understanding large-scale structure
* **Harmony** — Chord progressions and key relationships
* **Technique** — Specific challenges and solutions
* **Interpretation** — Stylistic considerations and musical decisions
* **Practice** — Targeted strategies for mastery
