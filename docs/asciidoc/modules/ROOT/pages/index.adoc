== Music and Mathematics

Music is deeply mathematical:

* **Rhythm** — ratios and subdivisions
* **Harmony** — frequency relationships (2:1 octave, 3:2 fifth)
* **Form** — symmetry, patterns, recursion
* **Counterpoint** — rule-based composition

== Music and Language

Music and language share:

* **Syntax** — rules of combination
* **Semantics** — meaning and emotion
* **Prosody** — rhythm and intonation
* **Grammar** — structure and hierarchy

== Approach

[arabic]
. **Listen actively** — analyze what you hear
. **Study theory** — understand the grammar
. **Analyze scores** — learn from masters
. **Compose** — apply knowledge
. **Produce** — bring ideas to life
